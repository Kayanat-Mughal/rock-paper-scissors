1.'rock' or 'r' - Choose rock
2.'paper' or 'p' - Choose paper
3.'scissors' or 's' - Choose scissors
4.'stats' - View game statistics
5.'quit' or 'q' - Exit game

-Rock beats Scissors
-Scissors beats Paper
-Paper beats Rock
-Same choice results in a tie
